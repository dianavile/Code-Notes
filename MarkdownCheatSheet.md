## c. EMPHASIS
To emphasize a text in markdown, use the following:
_ITALIC_:
- **:  *This text is italic* 
- _ _: _This text is italic__
__BOLD__:
- **This text is bold**
- __This text is bold__
(**NOTE:** **Combine** them as __you__ like**)
